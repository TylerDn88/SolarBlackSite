Experience SolarBlack, a thrilling VR/XR game where you slice, shoot, box and move to the beat. Take on an otherworldly threat while exploring stunning new worlds, all set to a handcrafted soundtrack that keeps you in the zone.

With Flextime, you control the pace of the action—go fast, go slow, or find your perfect rhythm. Uncover the secrets of the universe, sharpen your skills, and become the ultimate legend of sound and space.

Ready to take on the beat and prove yourself? Let’s play!

Thirty six worlds

36 unique worlds (environments), 24 Songs and 5 difficulties mean there is something for every legend or legend-in-training.

Easy to pick up

tons to train for and explore. Are you up for the challenge of unlocking all 16 achievements?

Slice and shoot

to the beat with your futuristic SaberGun, weaving two awesome fighting styles together in pulse-pounding, unstoppable rhythmic action.

Box the Beat

BOXING MODE is here to test your reflexes with jabs, hooks, and uppercuts—all to the beat! Plus, your stance will now dynamically switch between orthodox and southpaw to give you a full-body workout.

Conquer the global leaderboards
