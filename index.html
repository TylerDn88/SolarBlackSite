SolarBlack Action Rythm Fitness

SolarBlack is a non-stop action rhythm fitness game that takes you into new spheres! Experience fantastic worlds, explore hidden caves, breathe the thin air of the stratosphere and catapult yourself into new galaxies.
Slice and shoot Sphereombs to the beat of the music. The energy is charged with blows. Unlock your slomotion superpower with which you can hit countless targets, in action filled moments ala Matrix.
